curse is live — an all-in-one Discord moderation bot. The first release ships
**21 commands** across four categories, all on the comma (`,`) prefix.

### Added

- **Moderation** — `,ban`, `,unban`, `,kick`, `,mute`, `,unmute`, `,warn`, `,warnings`, `,purge`, `,nuke`, `,jail`, `,role`, `,forcenick`, `,unforcenick`.
- **Core** — `,userinfo`, `,serverinfo`, `,help`.
- **Verification** — `,setupverify`, `,restore`.
- **Dev** — `,cog`, `,eval`, `,botinfo`.

### Coming soon

- **Economy** and **Fun** modules — balances, dailies, shops, games, and more.
